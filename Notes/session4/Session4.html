Session 4: Projects

Learning Outcomes

Session 4 is the final session of the course. In this session, you will choose your final modelling project. This will give you the opportunity to apply the skills you have acquired during the previous sessions to real-world problems.

You will select your project depending on your cohort, with one student per project.

Contents

Project lists:

  • MSc Analytical Chemistry
  • MSc Materials Chemistry
  • MSc Medicinal and Biological Chemistry

Writing your report

Your final project work will be evaluated based on the report that you hand in. We are providing you with templates for Microsoft Word and Overleaf. We strongly encourage you to choose the Overleaf template and become familiar with writing in LaTeX.
Explanations on expected content, structure and word counts are provided in the documents.
