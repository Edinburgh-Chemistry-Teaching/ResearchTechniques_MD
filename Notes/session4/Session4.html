Session 4: Projects

Learning Outcomes

Session 4 is the final session of the course. In this session, your modelling project will be discussed and allocated. This will give you the opportunity to apply the skills you have acquired during the previous sessions to real-world problems.

You will select your project depending on your cohort, with one student per project.

Writing your report

Your final project work will be evaluated based on the report that you hand in. The project should be submitted as a PDF file. We are providing you with templates for Microsoft Word and Overleaf. We strongly encourage you to choose the Overleaf template and become familiar with writing in LaTeX. Explanations on expected content, structure and word counts are provided in the documents.

  • Microsoft Word template
  • OverLeaf LaTex template

Using an OverLeaf Template

  1. Register with your University email at Overleaf.com
  2. Click on the link to the OverLeaf Template; you will be prompted to ‘Join the Project’, click yes, and it will open in your browser.
  3. This is a template and is not editable.
  4. To make an editable copy, click on the ‘Menu’ (top left)
  5. Select ‘Copy Project’ and give it a name, click ‘Copy’
  6. The project will now appear in your project list
  7. Click on the project to open it. You can now edit the text. Make sure to click compile to see the changes appear on the PDF
  8. When you are ready to submit, you can download a PDF (make sure to do a final recompile!)
